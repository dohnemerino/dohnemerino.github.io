More Than A Horse

Who are we? Where are we going in life? What is the meaning of all of this? These are questions Li'l Sebastian never had to answer, because he was a horse.

Lil sebastian was the best mini horse that ever walked this great planet. When something is so terrible that it makes a Swanson cry, we know the loss of our great furry friend is beyond unbearable for us mere mortals. We miss you everyday buddy, and hope you are enjoying your wings up in horsy heaven.
